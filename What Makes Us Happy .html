What makes us happy?

Is there a connection between our well-being and economic/social measures such as GDP?

This scatterplot plots the well-being of 22 European countries against 10 different measures. Start by clicking the measures above and see what trends you can identify.

Well-being data is from the New Economics Foundation's National Accounts of Well-being and the category data is from Gapminder.
